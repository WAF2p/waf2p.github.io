The 7 pillars of WAF++

WAF++ structures cloud architecture across seven pillars — from Security to Sovereign. This creates a vendor-neutral framework that brings together multi-cloud reality, sovereignty, and regulatory requirements.

  • Security
  • Cost Optimization
  • Performance Efficiency
  • Reliability
  • Operational Excellence
  • Sustainability
  • Sovereign
