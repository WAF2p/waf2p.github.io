Framework

The 7 Pillars

The WAF++ framework is built on seven foundational pillars for cloud, platform, and governance.

Status

Currently available: Pillar 2 & Pillar 7

We are starting intentionally lean: Cost Optimization and Sovereign are the first fully developed pillars. The remaining pillars will follow step by step. If you'd like to contribute: feedback and contributions are welcome.
