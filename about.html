About us

WAF++ is a community-driven, cloud-agnostic framework built from real-world experience. We create a shared foundation for sovereign, responsible, and traceable cloud adoption — independent of providers and beyond marketing narratives.

OUR MISSION

Engineering-first — not a best-practices collection.

We bring together experience from architects, platform teams, security, and compliance specialists in an open, transparent, and neutral framework.

A shared lens for cloud decisions

WAF++ turns fragmented cloud advice into one coherent system. Every control maps to a real architectural question so teams can decide with confidence.

HISTORY

How WAF++ has grown

From the first sketches to a thriving community: WAF++ is built iteratively — shaped by project reality, discussions, and the drive to make cloud decisions traceable.

Start: Clarifying the problem

We collected common questions from platform and architecture teams and structured them into a shared lens.

Structure: Principles & guardrails

Experience turned into repeatable building blocks: responsibilities, decision paths, and maturity.

Community: Review & feedback

Through reviews, workshops, and conversations, content was refined — with a focus on neutrality and practicality.

Today: Continuous evolution

WAF++ stays open: new contributions, new perspectives, continuous improvements — transparent and usable day to day.
